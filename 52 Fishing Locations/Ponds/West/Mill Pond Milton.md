# Mill Pond — Milton

**Address:** 61 Garden Lane, Milton, ON L9T 2P7
**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~50 km / 40–50 min (Hwy 427 S to QEW/403 toward Milton)
**Managed by:** Public park — Town of Milton / Halton Sportsmen's Association (derby)

## Access Point

| Location | Maps |
|---|---|
| Mill Pond — downtown Milton | [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Mill+Pond+61+Garden+Lane+Milton+Ontario) |

## Season & Fees

| | Details |
|---|---|
| **Open Season** | Trout: 4th Saturday in April (derby only, put-and-take) · Warmwater species (bass, pike, panfish): year-round |
| **Admission** | Free — public park, no gate |
| **Licence Required** | Yes — valid Ontario fishing licence (18+) |

## Stocking

| Species | Schedule | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Rainbow trout | Once per year — stocked immediately before the spring Children's Fishing Derby (late April) | Put-and-take for derby only; pond does not sustain trout year-round (warm water) |
| Largemouth bass, northern pike, pumpkinseed, carp, perch | Resident — no stocking needed | Year-round warmwater fishing |

## Special Rules

- No live bait (Conservation Halton / Southern Bait Management Zone)
- FMZ 16 regulations apply
- Urban public park — no CA gate or permit required
