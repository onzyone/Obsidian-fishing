# Flesherton Community Pond

**Address:** 54 Durham St, Flesherton, ON N0C 1E0
**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~160 km / 1h 45 min – 2h (Hwy 410/10 N through Brampton, Orangeville, up Hwy 10 to Flesherton)
**Managed by:** Municipality of Grey Highlands

## Season & Fees

| | Details |
|---|---|
| **Open Season** | 4th Saturday in April – September 30 (2026: Apr 25 – Sep 30) · Park open year-round |
| **Admission** | Free — public municipal park |
| **Licence Required** | Yes — valid Ontario fishing licence |

## Stocking

| Species | Schedule | Recent Stocking Numbers |
|---|---|---|
| Brook trout | Once per year — spring, before season opener | 2022: 1,325 · 2023: 1,503 · 2024: 1,250 · **2025: 5,263** (major increase) |
| Smallmouth bass, largemouth bass, yellow perch, rock bass | Resident — no stocking | Year-round warmwater fishing |

> **Note:** The 2025 stocking of 5,263 brook trout is a significant jump from prior years (~1,250–1,500) — should make for an excellent season.

## Special Rules

- FMZ 16 regulations apply
- Swimming area at the same pond (weekly water testing June–September) — shared use with beach
- No specific local rules published beyond provincial regulations
