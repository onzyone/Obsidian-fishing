# Rouge River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~35–40 min
**Species:** Steelhead, salmon, rainbow trout, pike (up to 20 lbs)
**Seasons:** Mar – Apr (steelhead); Sept – Oct (salmon)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season |
|---|---|---|
| Below Hwy 407 → Lake Ontario | No | Year-round |
| Above Hwy 407 | No | Apr 25 – Sept 30 (FMZ 16) |

- No specific C&R zones — standard FMZ 16 rules apply

## Notes

- Rouge National Urban Park — protected land, great scenery
- Mix of urban and semi-wild water
- Pike fishing in lower sections can be exceptional
