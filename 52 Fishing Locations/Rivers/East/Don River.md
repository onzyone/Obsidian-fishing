# Don River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~20–25 min
**Species:** Steelhead, salmon, bass, pike, carp
**Seasons:** Spring run (Mar – Apr); Fall run (Sept – Oct)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season |
|---|---|---|
| Below Eglinton Ave → Lake Ontario | No | Year-round |
| Above Eglinton Ave | No | Apr 25 – Sept 30 (FMZ 16) |

- No specific C&R zones — standard FMZ 16 rules apply

## Notes

- Flows through eastern Toronto — very accessible urban fishery
- Not the most pristine water but produces fish
- Good for beginners due to proximity
