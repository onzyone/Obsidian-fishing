# Soper Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~60–70 min
**Species:** Steelhead (rainbow trout), Chinook salmon, brown trout
**Seasons:** Spring (Mar–May), Fall (Oct–Nov)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lower section (near mouth) | No | Standard | Year-round (verify annually) |
| Upper sections | No | Standard | Apr 25 – Dec 31 (FMZ 17) |
| Near fish ladder (sanctuary) | — | — | Verify sanctuary boundaries annually |

- Standard limits apply on all open sections
- Verify sanctuary sections near the fish ladder annually before fishing

## Notes

- Flows through Bowmanville/Clarington area into Lake Ontario
- Gets steelhead and salmon runs in spring and fall
- Small to medium creek; manageable wading
- Some public access near the mouth at Bowmanville (Clarington) waterfront
- Often fished alongside or confused with Bowmanville Creek, one valley to the west
- Lower fishing pressure than more well-known eastern Lake Ontario tributaries
- Verify sanctuary sections near the fish ladder each season — boundaries can change
