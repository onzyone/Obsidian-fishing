# Cobourg Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~75–85 min
**Species:** Steelhead, Chinook salmon, brown trout
**Seasons:** Spring & Fall

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|
| **Lamprey barrier → King St Bridge** | — | **CLOSED year-round** (sanctuary) |
| CNR right-of-way → Lake Ontario | No | Year-round (open all year) |
| Above CNR right-of-way | No | Standard FMZ 17 |

- No C&R zones — standard limits apply outside sanctuary
- Known in regulations as "**Cobourg Brook**"

## Notes

- Located near the town of Cobourg, ~100 km east of Toronto
- Cold, clean water — good quality fishery
- Upper sections mostly private property; lower river has decent public access
- Two forks both covered by Cobourg Conservation Area
