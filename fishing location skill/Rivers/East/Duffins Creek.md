# Duffins Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~45–50 min
**Species:** Steelhead, Chinook salmon, brook trout (above dam), brown trout
**Seasons:** Spring & Fall runs; brook trout above dam in summer

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|
| Lower section | No | Apr 25 – Aug 31; **CLOSED** Sept 1 – Oct 14; Oct 15 – Dec 31 |
| Upper (above dam) | No | Standard FMZ 17 |

- No C&R zones
- **Gap in season**: lower river closed Sept 1 – Oct 14 — do not fish during this window

## Notes

- Ajax / Pickering area — east of Toronto
- Dam on West Duffins Creek acts as barrier — steelhead and salmon stack below
- Above dam: good brook trout fishing in summer
- East fork: Trans-Canada Trail for long walking access

## MNR Brook Trout Data

*Source: Atlas of Brook Trout Streams and Rivers in Ontario, Ontario MNR (2003)*

| MNR District | County | Township | Lat | Long | Watershed |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Aurora | Durham | Pickering | 43°48' | 79°02' | 2HC-10 |

- Brook trout found in both east and west branches
- Sources: FISHNET, AHIS, HABPROG, CMN, Durham Region (1992)
- Ganatsekiagon Creek (NL) is a tributary of Duffins Creek between Whitevale and Urfe Creek branch (also brook trout, same watershed 2HC-10)
