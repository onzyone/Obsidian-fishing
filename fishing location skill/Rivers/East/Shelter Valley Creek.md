# Shelter Valley Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~80–90 min
**Species:** Steelhead, Chinook salmon, brown trout
**Seasons:** Spring & Fall

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season |
|---|---|---|
| General | No | Apr 25 – Dec 31 (FMZ 17) |

- No specific C&R zones — standard FMZ 17 rules apply

## Notes

- Small creek (~12 ft wide) near Grafton, Northumberland County
- Healthy populations of steelhead, salmon, and trout for its size
- **Access is the main challenge** — most of the creek runs through private property
- Worth it if you can get access; low pressure fishery
