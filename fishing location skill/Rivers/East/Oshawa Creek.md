# Oshawa Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~40–50 min
**Species:** Steelhead, Chinook salmon, brown trout, brook trout
**Seasons:** Spring (Mar – May steelhead); Fall (Sept – Nov salmon & steelhead)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season |
|---|---|---|
| General | No | Apr 25 – Dec 31 (FMZ 17) |

- No specific C&R zones — standard FMZ 17 rules apply

## Notes

- Flows through the town of Oshawa — urban lower sections, nicer water as you go upstream
- Large runs of steelhead and salmon make it one of the better east-GTA creeks
- Brook and brown trout present in upper sections year-round
- Good access through town parks and conservation areas
