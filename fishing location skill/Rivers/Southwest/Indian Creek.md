# Indian Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~1h 35–45 min (Niagara Peninsula)
**Species:** Chinook salmon (fall), white sucker, creek chub; rainbow trout/steelhead possible (limited data)
**Seasons:** Standard FMZ 16 season unless within a named Lake Ontario trib section

## Regulations

> ⚠️ **Exact watershed / location needs verification.** Fishbrain locates Indian Creek in the "Niagara Falls, Ontario" area, but multiple small creeks in the Niagara Peninsula carry this name. Verify with NPCA and current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary/fisheries-management-zone-16).

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season / Sanctuary |

| Creek (default) | No | Standard | **4th Sat April – Sept 30 (FMZ 16 default)** |

- Indian Creek is **not** specifically named in the FMZ 16 Lake Ontario tributary year-round exceptions (which cover Fifteen Mile, Forty Mile, Sixteen Mile, Twenty Mile, etc.), so assume standard zone season unless verified otherwise
- CA: **Niagara Peninsula Conservation Authority (NPCA)**

## Notes

- Small Niagara Peninsula creek in the Niagara Falls area per Fishbrain reports; exact mouth and watershed not well documented online
- Chinook salmon presence suggests it's a Lake Ontario or Niagara River tributary with fall spawning migration
- Very limited public information — low documented fishing pressure
- Likely a minor secondary option; serious Niagara anglers focus on the Niagara River, Twenty Mile Creek, or Twelve Mile Creek instead
- **Access, parking, and regulations should be verified on the ground** before a planned trip
