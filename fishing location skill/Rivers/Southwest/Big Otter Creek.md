# Big Otter Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~2h 15 min
**Species:** Steelhead, brown trout
**Seasons:** Spring (Mar–May), Fall (Oct–Dec)
**Conservation Authority:** Long Point Region CA (LPRCA) — 519-842-4242
**FMZ:** 16 (southwestern Ontario)

---

## Overview

Flows through Elgin County to Lake Erie at Port Burwell. A secondary steelhead option — confirmed runs in fall and spring but the watershed runs dirty/turbid after rain (agricultural drainage). Lower-pressure than Lake Ontario tribs. Big Creek (~30 min further west) is generally considered the better option, but Big Otter Creek is a reasonable alternative or backup.

---

## Regulations — FMZ 16

> ⚠️ Always verify at [ontario.ca FMZ 16](https://www.ontario.ca/page/sport-fishing-variation-order-fisheries-management-zone-16)

| Section | Open Season | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Bayham Township section (lower — near Port Burwell)** | All year (no close time) | Year-round open, S-5, C-2 |
| **Tillsonburg section (Black's Dam to Oxford St bridge)** | 4th Sat April – Dec 31 | Sanctuary Jan 1 – Friday before 4th Sat April |

Limit: S-5, C-2 standard; S-1, C-0 Oct–Dec on non-extended sections.

## Practical Notes

- Runs dirty/turbid fast after rain — time visits to falling, clearing flows
- Lower Bayham section is most accessible and is open year-round
- Forum reports confirm steelhead push in fall when water cools below ~55°F
- Big Creek is a better-documented, higher-quality option at ~30 min more drive
