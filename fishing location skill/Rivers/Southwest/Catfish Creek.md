# Catfish Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~2h 15 min
**Species:** Steelhead (limited, spring only); stocked rainbow trout at Springwater CA pond (May)
**Seasons:** Spring only
**Conservation Authority:** Catfish Creek Conservation Authority (CCCA) — 519-773-9037
**FMZ:** 16 (southwestern Ontario)

---

## Overview

Two distinct fisheries share this name — don't confuse them:

1. **Springwater Conservation Area pond** — stocked rainbow trout, May only. Excellent for beginners and families. Easy access, no wading required.
2. **Catfish Creek river (Port Bruce)** — may receive a limited steelhead push in spring. Not well-documented as a steelhead river; the lower section near Port Bruce is the target area.

Note: Bradleys Creek, a tributary of Catfish Creek, has a documented fish sanctuary — do not fish sanctuary sections.

## Practical Notes

- If targeting steelhead, confirm the creek is receiving fish before making the drive — limited trip reports available
- Springwater CA pond is a reliable alternative for a guaranteed catch (May stocking)
- Big Creek or Big Otter Creek are better steelhead options in this region
