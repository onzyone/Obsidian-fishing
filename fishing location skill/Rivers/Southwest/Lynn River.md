# Lynn River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~1h 30 min
**Species:** Brown trout (resident), steelhead (limited), some salmon
**Seasons:** Spring (Mar–May), Fall (Oct–Dec)
**Conservation Authority:** Long Point Region CA (LPRCA) — 519-842-4242
**FMZ:** 16 (southwestern Ontario)

---

## Overview

Small agricultural stream flowing into Lake Erie at Port Dover. Primary fishery is **resident brown trout** with a modest steelhead push in spring and fall. Smaller and lower-gradient than Big Creek — runs murky after rain and can be low in summer. Lower-pressure fishery but limited by stream size and access.

---

## Practical Notes

- Small stream — low water and warm in summer, not ideal Jul–Aug
- Agricultural watershed — clarity suffers after rain; wait for clearing flows
- Access is primarily at road crossings; surrounding land is private
- Big Creek (~25 min further west) is a significantly better steelhead option
