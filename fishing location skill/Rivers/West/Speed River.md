# Speed River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~1h – 1h 10 min
**Species:** Wild brown trout, rainbow trout
**Seasons:** Year-round; best spring and early summer for browns

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season |
|---|---|---|
| General | No | Apr 25 – Sept 30 (FMZ 16) |

- No specific C&R zones — standard FMZ 16 rules apply
- Landlocked above Guelph dam — no migratory fish, resident trout only

## Notes

- Flows through **Guelph** — Grand River tributary
- Excellent wild brown trout population
- Smaller, technical water — well-suited to fly fishing
- Urban sections through Guelph are surprisingly productive

## Access Points

> Below Guelph dam: **warmwater only** (bass, pike, carp, panfish) — no migratory or resident trout. Above Guelph Lake CA: brook trout, smallmouth bass, white sucker. Steelhead were extirpated below dam decades ago; restoration efforts ongoing as of 2026 but no confirmed re-establishment.
