# Mary Fix Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~25–30 min
**Species:** Chinook salmon (fall run up from Credit River mouth), creek chub, carp, white sucker; migratory browns/rainbow possible
**Seasons:** **Year-round lower section** (as a named Credit River tributary below Hwy 403)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Mary Fix Creek is a Credit River tributary, which means it falls under the FMZ 16 exception: **Credit River tributaries (Mississauga) — from the south side of Hwy 403 bridge downstream to Lake Ontario are open all year** for Atlantic salmon, brown trout, Pacific salmon, rainbow trout. Verify at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary/fisheries-management-zone-16).

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hwy 403 → Credit River confluence (Port Credit) | No | Standard | **Year-round (FMZ 16 Credit trib exception)** |
| Above Hwy 403 | No | Standard | 4th Sat April – Sept 30 (FMZ 16 default) |

- Lower section inherits Credit River lower-river regulations
- CA: **Credit Valley Conservation (CVC)**

## Notes

- Small urban tributary of the Credit River — rises near Mavis Rd and Eglinton Ave W, flows south through central Mississauga, Gordon Woods, Mineola West
- **Empties into the Credit River at the CN bridge in Port Credit** (not directly into Lake Ontario)
- Named after Mary Fix, former reeve of Toronto Township
- Very small creek — chinook salmon push up during fall Credit River run; most fish use Mary Fix as a side-channel spawning area
- Recently reconstructed along the **Hazel McCallion LRT (Line) corridor** — habitat improvements ongoing
- Not a destination fishery — more of a "side spot" off the main Credit mouth fishing
