# 16 Mile Creek (Sixteen Mile Creek)

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~45 min
**Species:** Steelhead, salmon
**Seasons:** Spring & Fall

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|
| Lakeshore → Lake Ontario | No | Year-round |
| **Hwy 2 → Hwy 407 (Oakville)** | — | **CLOSED** Jan 1 – Fri before 4th Sat April AND Oct 1 – Dec 31 (sanctuary) |
| Above Hwy 407 | No | Apr 25 – Dec 31 |

- No catch and release zones — standard limits apply outside sanctuary

## Notes

- Oakville area
- One of the better small-to-medium tributaries west of Toronto
- Less pressured than Credit or Humber
