# Shaw's Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~55 min
**Species:** Brown trout (resident), some rainbow trout early spring
**Seasons:** Spring & early summer (Apr 25 – June; fish by July as temps rise)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season |
|---|---|---|---|
| Entire creek (FMZ 16, Credit watershed) | ✅ **C&R only** | Artificial lures only · 1 single barbless hook | Apr 25 – Sept 30 |

- Falls within the **Credit River special regulation zone** (above Old Baseline Rd) — same C&R and artificial-only rules apply
- **Closed Oct 1 – Apr 24** — verify current regs annually

## Why Fish This Over the Main Credit

Shaw's Creek is a **spring-fed limestone tributary** that clears significantly faster than the main Credit after rain events. When the main Credit is blown out and murky:

- Shaw's Creek drains a much smaller, mostly forested/agricultural watershed
- Groundwater inputs maintain baseflow clarity
- Clearing time: **1–2 days after a rain event** vs. 3–5 days for the main Credit

**Best case to go here:** Main Credit gauge (02HB029) is rising/murky; you want brown trout on flies or spinners; you're willing to walk 20–40 min from the road allowance.

## Notes

- Small stream — typically 5–10 ft wide; tight casting quarters; short leader / small rod ideal
- Wild **resident brown trout** only — no steelhead or salmon (too far upstream of Norval Dam)
- Water temperatures typically exceed 22°C by mid-July — fish early mornings only in summer or wait for fall
- Mix of public road allowances and private farmland — stay on road allowances and marked public land
- Low fishing pressure — most Credit anglers don't know it exists or skip it for the bigger water

## Run Timing

- No migratory run — resident fish year-round (when open)
- Best: **late April through June** when temps are cold and flows up after snowmelt
- Falls as a summer stream deteriorates in heat — switch to West Credit or main upper Credit after July

## Access Points

| Location | Parking | Walk | Water / Notes | Maps |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Alton Village area (Hwy 10 / Mountainview Rd) | Roadside pulloff | 5–20 min upstream | Brook runs through farmland north of Alton; road allowance crossing at Mountainview Rd gives walk-in access upstream and down | [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Shaw%27s+Creek+Alton+Ontario) |
| Airport Road crossings (south of Orangeville) | Roadside pulloff | Walk upstream or downstream from crossing | Multiple rural road crossings along Shaw's Creek south of Orangeville — each is a legal access point | [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Shaw%27s+Creek+Caledon+Ontario) |

> Most land adjacent to Shaw's Creek is **private farmland** — access limited to road allowances at bridge/culvert crossings. Walk the road allowance (20m either side of road centerline) and ask permission if approaching private property.
