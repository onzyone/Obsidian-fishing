# West Credit River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~45–50 min
**Species:** Wild brown trout, wild brook trout
**Seasons:** Spring & Summer (Apr 25 – Sept 30); no migratory run — resident fish only

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season |
|---|---|---|---|
| Entire river (FMZ 16) | ✅ **C&R only** | Artificial lures only · 1 single barbless hook | Apr 25 – Sept 30 |

- Falls within the **Credit River special regulation zone** — same rules as upper Credit above Old Baseline Rd
- No bait permitted anywhere on the river
- **Closed Oct 1 – Apr 24** (verify current regs — sanctuary period applies)

## Notes

- Cold, spring-fed limestone tributary of the Credit River — joins the main Credit near Belfountain/Cataract
- Flows through the town of **Erin** (Wellington County) and south through Hillsburgh and Cataract
- One of the most visually "pretty" small streams in southern Ontario — clear water, mossy banks, classic small-stream character
- Supports **self-sustaining wild brown trout and brook trout** — no migratory steelhead or salmon (too small, too far from lake)
- Brook trout concentrate in the **colder upper sections closer to Cataract Falls** — the falls act as a temperature/habitat dividing line; browns dominate below
- Best fished with small spinners, small streamers, or dry flies — tight quarters in many sections
- Mix of public road allowances and private land — stick to road allowances and verify access
- Lower pressure than the main Credit; fly anglers and light-spin anglers occasionally encounter it via YouTube/social media

## Run Timing

- No migratory run — resident fish year-round (when open)
- Best fishing: **late April through June** when flows are up and temps are cold
- Summer low water can concentrate fish but also makes them spooky — early morning fishing recommended

## Hatch Chart

Same hatch timing as the Upper Credit River — see [[Rivers/West/Credit River#Hatch Chart — Upper Credit River|Credit River Hatch Chart]] for the full table. Isonychia (June–July), Hendrickson (May), and BWO (April/August) are the key hatches. Terrestrials (ants, grasshoppers) are productive through summer.
