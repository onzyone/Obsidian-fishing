# Etobicoke Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~20–30 min
**Species:** Steelhead (rainbow trout), Chinook salmon, brown trout
**Seasons:** Spring (Mar–May), Fall (Oct–Nov)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lakeshore to Lake Ontario (lower) | No | Standard | Year-round |
| Above Lakeshore (upper) | No | Standard | Apr 25 – Dec 31 (FMZ 16) |

- Standard limits apply on all open sections
- C&R recommended given urban watershed and water quality concerns

## Notes

- Small urban creek flowing through Etobicoke south to Lake Ontario
- Gets steelhead and salmon runs in spring and fall
- Much smaller and more urban than Humber River, one valley to the east
- Banks heavily developed in lower reaches — limited natural corridor
- Limited public access; best access near the mouth at Marie Curtis Park
- Water clarity often poor after rain due to urban runoff — fish still present but challenging
- Not heavily documented; low fishing pressure compared to nearby Humber and Credit
- C&R recommended given urban watershed conditions

## Access Points

> Creek flows Caledon → Brampton → Mississauga → Lake Ontario. Upper Brampton/Caledon sections better character for trout than the urbanized lower reaches.

| Location | Notes | Maps |
|---|---|---|
| **Marie Curtis Park** (mouth — primary lower access, gauged 02HC030 "Etobicoke Below QEW") | Best public access; parking at park; straddles Etobicoke/Mississauga boundary; fresh-run fish enter here | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Marie+Curtis+Park+Etobicoke+Ontario) |
| **Centennial Park (Mississauga)** (mid — urban) | Park area with creek access; mid-creek urban water; free parking | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Centennial+Park+Mississauga+Etobicoke+Creek) |
| **Eringate Park** (lower-mid — urban) | Etobicoke valley park access; trail connections | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Eringate+Park+Etobicoke+Toronto) |
| **Etobicoke Valley Park (Sherway/Dundas area)** (lower-mid) | Lower-mid creek park; gauge 02HC030 nearby; trail and bridge access | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Etobicoke+Valley+Park+Toronto) |
| **Brampton — Chinguacousy Park area** (upper-mid, gauged 02HC017) | Brampton city park with creek access; upper creek has better water character (resembles upper Credit) | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Chinguacousy+Park+Brampton+Ontario) |
| **Heart Lake Conservation Area (Brampton)** (upper — TRCA paid park) | TRCA park with creek access; resident trout water | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Heart+Lake+Conservation+Area+Brampton+Ontario) |
| **Caledon / Mayfield Rd crossings** (upper — Caledon road allowances) | Upper creek road allowances; better trout water | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Etobicoke+Creek+Caledon+Ontario) |
