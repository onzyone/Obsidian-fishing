# Mimico Creek

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~15–20 min
**Species:** Chinook salmon (fall), migratory brown trout, rainbow trout/steelhead (limited), smallmouth bass, largemouth bass, northern pike (mouth), white sucker, pumpkinseed, yellow perch, carp
**Seasons:** Mouth/Humber Bay year-round for warmwater; creek itself standard FMZ 16 season

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Mimico Creek is **not** named in FMZ 16 Lake Ontario tributary exceptions — defaults to standard zone season for trout/salmon. Always verify at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary/fisheries-management-zone-16).

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Entire creek (trout / salmon) | No | Standard | **4th Sat April – Sept 30 (FMZ 16 default)** — no legal fall run season |
| Mouth / Humber Bay lake waters (bass, pike, panfish, carp) | No | Standard | Bass: 4th Sat June – Nov 30 · Pike: open all year · Sunfish/perch/carp: open all year |
| Lake Ontario boundary waters (trout/salmon) | No | Standard | Open all year in Lake Ontario itself |

- Fall salmon and steelhead do enter the creek, but trout/salmon season on the creek closes Sept 30 — fish legally at the mouth in Humber Bay (lake waters) during the fall run
- CA: **Toronto and Region Conservation Authority (TRCA)**

## Notes

- Small urban creek — headwaters near Pearson Airport, flows south through Etobicoke and empties at **Humber Bay Park** (splits East and West Humber Bay parks)
- **Primary fishery is the mouth at Humber Bay**, not the creek itself — fall Chinook, brown trout, and some steelhead stage here before pushing into the Humber
- Large white sucker spawning run in Mimico Creek draws pike to shallow waters at the mouth in spring
- Urban creek — water clarity poor after rain, stormwater-driven
- Not a serious destination on its own; handy convenience spot for Toronto-west anglers
