# Sauble River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~2h 15–30 min
**Species:** Steelhead (rainbow trout), some brown trout
**Seasons:** Spring (Mar–May), Fall (Oct–Nov)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lower section (mouth/Sauble Beach) | No | Standard | Extended fall / year-round — verify FMZ 23 |
| Upper sections | No | Standard | 3rd Saturday in May to Sept 30 (standard FMZ 23) |

- Standard limits apply on all open sections
- FMZ 23 (Lake Huron zone) season dates differ from FMZ 16 — verify current dates annually before fishing
- Extended fall season on lower section — confirm in current regulations

## Notes

- Flows into Lake Huron at Sauble Beach
- A Lake Huron tributary — different fishery zone (FMZ 23) than Lake Ontario or Georgian Bay tribs
- Steelhead enter from Lake Huron in spring and fall
- Sauble Beach is a popular tourist destination — fishing pressure can be higher near the mouth in spring
- The upper Sauble holds resident brook trout in cold headwater sections
- Scenic sand dunes and beach environment near the mouth
