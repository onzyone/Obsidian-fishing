# Bighead River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~1h 50 min – 2h
**Species:** Wild steelhead (rainbow trout), brown trout
**Seasons:** Spring (Mar–May), Fall (Oct–Dec)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lower section (mouth/Meaford area) | No | Standard | Year-round (FMZ 16) |
| Upper sections | No | Standard | Standard season (FMZ 16) |

- Standard limits apply on all open sections
- Verify current regs annually — lower section year-round designation should be confirmed

## Notes

- Flows into Georgian Bay at Meaford
- Part of the Georgian Bay trib cluster (Beaver, Bighead, Sydenham) that receive excellent wild steelhead runs
- The Bighead Valley is known for cold, clear water and scenic Niagara Escarpment setting
- Meaford harbour mouth and lower river are the primary access points
- Lower fishing pressure than Credit or Humber — Georgian Bay tribs are less known among Toronto-area anglers
- Wild fish — not heavily stocked; fish hard in cold Georgian Bay-fed water
