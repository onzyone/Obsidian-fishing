# Noisy River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~1h 20–30 min
**Species:** Wild brown trout, brook trout
**Seasons:** Spring & summer (resident trout)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season |
|---|---|---|
| General | No | Apr 25 – Sept 30 (FMZ 16) |

- No specific C&R zones — standard FMZ 16 rules apply

## Notes

- Near **Dunedin / Creemore** area — Nottawasaga headwaters
- Outstanding wild trout population — one of the best small streams in Ontario
- Pristine cold water, tight brushy banks — good for short-line nymphing or dry fly
- Fishes best in May–July for rises
