# Maitland River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~2h – 2h 15 min
**Species:** Steelhead (rainbow trout), brown trout, Chinook salmon
**Seasons:** Spring (Mar–May), Fall (Oct–Nov)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Gear Rules | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lower section (Goderich / river mouth area) | No | Standard | Extended or year-round — verify FMZ 23 |
| Upper sections | No | Standard | Standard season (FMZ 23) |

- Standard limits apply on all open sections
- Verify lower section extended/year-round designation in current FMZ 23 regulations annually

## Notes

- Flows into Lake Huron at Goderich
- Part of the Lake Huron trib cluster alongside the Saugeen River
- Large, agricultural river — water can be murky/turbid after rain; fish still present but visibility reduced
- Goderich harbour and lower river near the lighthouse/pier area is the prime steelhead access zone
- Known for big fish entering from Lake Huron
- Huron County Conservation Authority manages access points
- Less celebrated than the Saugeen but a good alternate option on the same Lake Huron trip
- Goderich town is scenic and worth a visit (famous for its octagonal town square)

## Access Points

> Steelhead range from Goderich mouth upstream to Wingham dam (no fish ladder — natural ceiling for migratory fish).

| Location | Notes | Maps |
|---|---|---|
| **Goderich Harbour / Lighthouse** (lower — primary, river mouth) | Near lighthouse/pier; primary steelhead access from Lake Huron; free harbour parking | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Goderich+Harbour+Marina+Ontario) |
| **Falls Reserve Conservation Area (Benmiller)** (lower-mid — gauged 02FE015) | MVCA-managed; campground + day-use; falls section below Benmiller; classic riffle/pool water; paid day-use | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Falls+Reserve+Conservation+Area+Ontario) |
| **Benmiller village bridge** (mid — Benmiller Line) | Bridge crossing in Benmiller village; guided fly operations based here (Fly Fitters); road-allowance access | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Benmiller+Inn+Ontario) |
| **Auburn — Hwy 8 Bridge** (mid) | Hwy 8 crossing in Auburn village; pull-off parking; mid-river holding water | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Auburn+Ontario+Huron+County) |
| **Wingham Dam** (upper barrier — fall staging) | Furthest upstream steelhead penetration — no fish ladder; fish stage below dam in fall; town parking | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Wingham+Lower+Town+Dam+Ontario) |
| **Belgrave road allowances** (upper) | Rural road crossings between Wingham and Auburn; resident brown trout water | [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Belgrave+Ontario+Huron+County) |
