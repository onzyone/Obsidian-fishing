# Pine River

**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~1h 20–30 min
**Species:** Wild brown trout, brook trout
**Seasons:** Spring & summer (resident trout)

## Regulations

> ⚠️ Always verify current regs at [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary) before fishing.

| Section | C&R? | Season / Sanctuary |
|---|---|---|
| General | No | Apr 25 – Sept 30 (FMZ 16) |
| Near Nottawasaga confluence | — | Nottawasaga sanctuary Oct 1 – Dec 31 applies near confluence — check boundary |

- No specific C&R zones on the Pine River itself

## Notes

- Flows through **Horning's Mills / Shelburne** area — Nottawasaga tributary
- Excellent wild brook and brown trout in upper reaches
- Smaller intimate stream — fly fishing or light spinning
- Part of the Nottawasaga headwaters network (also check [[Noisy River]] nearby)
