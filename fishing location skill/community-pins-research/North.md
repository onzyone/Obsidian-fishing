## Summary

- **Total: ~30 pins** across 7 of 8 rivers (Innisfil = none).
- **Richest signal:** Bighead River — Legion Hole (named, multi-source), Beautiful Joe Park (named GMaps place + civic page), fully-documented Trout Hollow Trail. Closest "313 fishing spot" equivalent: **"Legion Hole" below Edwin Street bridge** in Meaford.
- **Runner-up:** Beaver River — three named fishways (Thornbury / Clendenan / Slabtown), three numbered Visit Grey access points, upper Hogg's Falls / Eugenia limits.
- **Thinnest:** Innisfil Creek (no community-named spots — confirms warmwater queue filter). Pine River concentrated almost entirely at Pine River PFA (Mulmur).
- **Noisy + Mad** well-covered by named conservation parks but few discrete "hole" pins.

## Caveats / regs

- **FMZ 16 inland trout/salmon Apr 25 – Sept 30** unless extended-season applies.
- **Beaver extended season**: Thornbury Dam → Georgian Bay, **4th Sat April – Dec 31**.
- **Sydenham**: fall sanctuary (Sept/Oct) N of Mill Dam to Inglis Falls; above-dam closed until **June 1** with special regs.
- **Boyne**: special fish sanctuary restrictions — verify before fishing.
- **Pine**: most posted private; Pine River PFA is main legal public access.
- **Noisy**: tight, hike-in only; **C&R** strongly encouraged.
- All approx lat/lons should be verified against satellite before pinning. Visit Grey + Beautiful Joe pins are authoritative.
- No raw Reddit threads surfaced — community signal lives on OTS, Hook Line & Sinker, A Perfect Drift, conservation-authority pages, Visit Grey.
