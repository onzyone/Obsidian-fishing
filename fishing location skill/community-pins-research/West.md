## Summary

- **Total: 14 pins** across 5 creeks (Etobicoke 2, Sixteen Mile 4, Bronte 4, Mimico 3, Cooksville 1).
- **Richest signal:** Bronte Creek (park-published access points + Petro Canada Park has its own Fishbrain entry as a named place + steelhead-specific community discussion). Sixteen Mile second richest with Lions Valley as the clear hub.
- **Weakest signal:** Cooksville (only mouth at R.K. McMillan). Etobicoke similarly weak — community says only mouth at Marie Curtis is fishable.
- **No "Google Maps named place" equivalents to Bowmanville's "313 fishing spot"** — closest analogues are Fishbrain-pinned named places (Petro Canada Park, Oakville Harbour, Humber Bay Park, R.K. McMillan Park) which are city/park names rather than angler-coined.

## Caveats

1. Reddit unreachable — there may be additional pins not surfaced.
2. Forum threads (Ontario Fishing Forums, Ontario Fishing Community, ontariotroutandsteelhead.com) returned 403 — pins derived from search-result snippets.
3. Approximate lat/lon flagged — verify with Google Maps before adding to canonical Access Points.
4. Mimico + Etobicoke close after Sept 30. Bronte extended season to Dec 31 north of Rebecca St.
5. Several pins likely overlap with canonical (Marie Curtis, Lions Valley, Petro Canada, Bronte Heritage, Humber Bay, R.K. McMillan, BCPP Lot A/F) — dedupe required.
