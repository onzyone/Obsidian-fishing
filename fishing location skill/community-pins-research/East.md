## Summary

- **Total: 36 pins** across 8 creeks (excluding "313 fishing spot" baseline).
- **Richest signal:** Oshawa Creek (7 pins, dense park-by-park trail signal) and Rouge River (6 pins, mouth-to-Milne corridor well documented).
- **Tied next:** Duffins Creek (7 pins, well-mapped multi-fork system) and Bowmanville (5 pins beyond baseline).
- **Sparsest:** Shelter Valley (only 2 viable — rest private/posted) and Wilmot (only 3 — Wilmot Club locks up most of river).
- **No new "named GMaps places"** of "313 fishing spot" caliber surfaced via web search — those user-named POIs are only discoverable by zooming GMaps directly.

## Caveats

- All lat/lon **approximate** unless noted. Treat as "good enough to drop a pin near" — not survey-grade.
- **Posted property recurring**: Wilmot (Wilmot Club + private bottom), Shelter Valley (entire reach below Hwy 401 private), Cobourg upper.
- **Don River pins** are mostly viewing/pressure spots, not honey holes — Pottery Rd has known foul-hooking + enforcement attention.
- **Reddit returned essentially zero indexed signal** for these creeks via Google site-search.
- **Sanctuary/seasonal closures** apply most creeks above CN/CNR line Sept/Oct → 4th Sat April. Confirm current MNRF/FMZ-17 regs.
