# Millbrook MNR Fishing & Recreation Area

**Address:** 772 Zion Line, Millbrook, ON L0A 1G0
**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~125 km / 1h 20 min – 1h 30 min (Hwy 115/35 N toward Peterborough, exit toward Millbrook)
**Managed by:** Ontario MNRF (provincially managed public area)

## Stocking

| Species | Schedule | Recent Stocking Numbers |
|---|---|---|
| Brook trout — **Pond 1** | Spring, before opener | 2022: 2,254 · 2023: 2,042 · 2024: 2,045 · 2025: 2,230 |
| Brook trout — **Pond 2** | Spring, before opener | 2022: 2,254 · 2023: 2,070 · 2024: 2,055 · 2025: 2,270 |

> Very consistent stocking (~2,000–2,300 per pond per year). Baxter Creek running through the site also holds wild trout. Annual Millbrook Fishing Derby held near the season opener.

## Special Rules

- FMZ 17 regulations apply: brook trout limit **2/day sport, 1 possession** · aggregate trout/salmon 5/day sport, 2 possession
- Southern Bait Management Zone — no live baitfish transported into/out of zone
- No vehicles or bicycles on the Valley Trails
- Two ponds accessible via the Grand Trunk Pathway from the Zion Line parking lot
