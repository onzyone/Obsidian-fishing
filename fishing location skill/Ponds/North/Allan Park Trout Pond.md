# Allan Park Trout Pond

**Address:** 301820 Concession Rd 2 SDR, Hanover, ON N4N 3B8
**Drive from 427 & Finch:** ~180 km / 2h – 2h 10 min (Hwy 400 N to Hwy 26/10, or Hwy 9/10 through Orangeville toward Hanover)
**Managed by:** Saugeen Valley Conservation Authority (SVCA)

## Access Point

| Location | Maps |
|---|---|
| Allan Park Conservation Area — trout pond | [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Allan+Park+Conservation+Area+Hanover+Ontario) |

## Season & Fees

| | Details |
|---|---|
| **Open Season** | 4th Saturday in April – September 30 (2026: Apr 25 – Sep 30) · Park open year-round for day use |
| **Admission** | **$5.00 parking fee** (pay online — single-day or annual pass) |
| **Fishing Fee** | None beyond parking |
| **Licence Required** | Yes — valid Ontario fishing licence (except on provincial licence-free weekend in July) |
| **Ice Fishing** | Prohibited |

## Stocking

| Species | Schedule | Recent Stocking Numbers |
|---|---|---|
| Brook trout | Spring, before opener | 2022: 5,185 fish |
| Brown trout | Spring, before opener | 2023: 2,069 · 2024: 2,824 · 2025: 2,287 |

> Put-and-take system — keeping fish is expected and encouraged. SVCA posts stocking announcements on their website.

## Special Rules

- FMZ 16 regulations apply: brook trout limit 2/day (sport), brown trout limit 5/day (sport)
- No ice fishing
- 162-ha forested conservation area with 15 km of trails
- Southern Bait Management Zone — no live baitfish transported into/out of zone
