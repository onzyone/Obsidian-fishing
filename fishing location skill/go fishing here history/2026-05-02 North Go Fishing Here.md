### What to Expect

- **Species:** Drop-back rainbow / brown trout (Beaver, Humber, Nottawasaga mouth, Saugeen); resident brown / brook trout (Mad, Bighead, Innisfil Creek, Boyne).
- **Beaver tactics:** Float roe through the runs below the fish ladder. Single eggs (chartreuse / pink), small spoons (Cleos #2), or stonefly nymphs. Drop-backs are aggressive — strip streamers also work.
- **Humber tactics:** Float fishing through Old Mill / Etienne Brulé pools — single egg bags, roe sacs, small spoons. Lake-mouth at Humber Bay for late stagers.
- **Small-stream tactics (Mad / Innisfil / Boyne):** 6'6"–7'6" UL/L spinning, 4lb mono. Mepps #0/1, Panther Martins, or worm under split shot. Light tackle; tight casting.
- **Gear (steelhead rivers):** 8'6"–10'6" ML steelhead rod, 6lb mainline, 6lb fluoro leader. Bring de-icer and a thermos.
- **Cautions:**
  - **Cold dawn (~0.1°C)** — ice on guides; thermos + gloves.
  - **Full moon overnight Sat → Sun** — softer first-light bite; push to 8–10 am for second wave.
  - **Beaver is popular** — arrive at first light or work the mouth/Heathcote if the ladder is shoulder-to-shoulder.
  - **Tue May 5 = 17.8mm rain blowout** — Sunday is the *only* clean day before the next blow-out.
- **Regulations:** Always verify on [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary). Lower year-round sections + FMZ 16/17 inland are all open.

## Notes

- **FMZ 17 inland trout regs:** open since Apr 25; standard creel limits. MNR brook-trout headwaters are low-density populations — consider barbless C&R.
- **Property lines:** road-allowance access only on most MNR brook trout pins. Stay in the road allowance, don't cross fences. Many upper sections are private.
- **Tackle swap:** small-stream brook trout = 6'6"–7'6" UL/L spinning, 4lb mono, small spinners (Mepps #0/1, Panther Martin) or worm under split shot. Steelhead rod is overkill upstream.
- **Sequencing for Sun May 3:** Hit Humber (Old Mill) at first light → bounce north to Innisfil Creek for resident browns mid-morning if main bite slows. Or commit to Beaver fish ladder for the full day if drop-backs are stacked. **Mid-distance alternative:** Essa Centennial Park (Utopia) on the Notty mainstem is ~1h 10 min — solid year-round-section pin pairs well with Innisfil Creek cluster (15 min apart).
