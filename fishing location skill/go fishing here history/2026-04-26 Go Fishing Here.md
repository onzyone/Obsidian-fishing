### What to Expect

- Drop-back steelhead, resident rainbow, brown trout, suckers. Spring run winding down — no fresh push expected.
- Cool water (likely 8–11°C lower sections by Sat). Slow your presentation — small egg patterns, single-egg rigs, small spoons / Cleos near mouths, worms under float in coloured water.
- Coloured water on Sat is a feature, not a bug, for small creeks: bigger profile, brighter colours (chartreuse, pink, orange).
- Dress cool — overnight lows near freezing Fri/Sat; bring layers and waders if going early.
- Sun May 3 light drizzle (0.6mm) won't blow anything; if Sat doesn't fit your schedule, Sun is a solid second.
