### What to Expect

Steelhead are the primary target. Fish will be stacking in pools and deeper runs post-rain flush — look for seams off fast water, inside bends, slower tail-outs. Sunday morning best before midday warming. Water will be coloured — go bright (pink eggs, flame/chartreuse streamers, pink San Juan worms). Float/drift rig in deeper pools; swing streamers through runs. Arrive early.
