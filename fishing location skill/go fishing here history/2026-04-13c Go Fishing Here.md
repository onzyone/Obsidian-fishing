### What to Expect

**Spring run status:** Early — just getting started. Ganaraska confirmed first fish Apr 12. The storm this week will actually push fish movement: a big rain event followed by dropping, clearing water is a classic trigger for steelhead to push upstream. You're fishing the start of the run, not the peak, but the post-flood window is often the most active.

**Tactics for post-flood conditions:** Water will be 7–10°C and slightly coloured even in the small creeks. Use bright attractor patterns — pink/chartreuse egg beads, spawn bags, orange/red jigs under a float. Fish will be stacked in the deeper pools and tail-outs, and in any current seams with slack water adjacent. Don't rush — let the float drift slow and deep. At Bowmanville, the stretch from the fish ladder south to the lake mouth is the prime zone.
