### What to Expect

Drop-back steelhead window is closing — bigger fish have mostly returned to the lake but a tail of post-spawn hens still slides through the small creek mouths on falling water. Resident browns active across all the smaller creeks, plus brookies if you push upstream into FMZ 17 inland (open since Apr 25). Tactics: small spinners (Mepps #1–2), drift-rigged roe bags or single eggs, tan/olive woolly buggers on a 4–6 wt fly rod. Wilmot mouth and Bowmanville fishway pool both get very clear at this flow — go finer leader (6–8lb fluoro) and longer drifts. With the 🌗 Last Quarter (~54% illum) the bite curve is standard — hit dawn patrol hard, evening as backup.
