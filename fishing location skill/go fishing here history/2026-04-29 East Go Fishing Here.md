### What to Expect

- **Species:** Drop-back steelhead at lake mouths; resident rainbow/brown trout; suckers stacking. Limited fishing on Bowmanville/Ganaraska this weekend.
- **Tactics for stained water:** Bright/scented baits — chartreuse or pink roe bags, fluorescent jigs, larger spinners (Mepps #3) for visibility. Float fishing through deeper holes where fish hold during high water.
- **Gear:** Same setup as standard steelhead — 8'6"–10'6" ML rod, 6lb mainline, 6lb fluoro leader. Bring de-icer.
- **Cautions:**
  - **Cold dawn (1.5°C Sun)** — ice on guides; thermos.
  - **Full moon** — softer dawn bite likely; mid-morning push.
  - **East zone compromised** — verify gauge readings Sat night before driving (Bowmanville 02HD006, Ganaraska 02HD012). If still rising, drive to West/North/Southwest instead.
  - **Sanctuaries** — Bowmanville fish ladder and Ganaraska Hwy 401→Jocelyn St Bridge.
- **Regulations:** Always verify on [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary). FMZ 17 lower sections all open since Apr 25.
