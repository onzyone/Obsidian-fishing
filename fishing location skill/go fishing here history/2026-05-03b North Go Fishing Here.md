### What to Expect

- **Species:** Drop-back rainbow / brown trout (Beaver, Humber, Notty mid + mouth, Saugeen); resident brown / brook trout (Mad, Bighead, Innisfil, Boyne, Pine, Sheldon, Sydenham); waking smallmouth at Humber + Beaver mouth as water hits 14°C+.
- **Beaver tactics:** Float roe through runs below the fish ladder. Single eggs (chartreuse / pink), small spoons (Cleos #2), stonefly nymphs. Streamers strip well in warming water.
- **Humber tactics:** Float fishing through Old Mill / Etienne Brulé pools — single egg bags, roe sacs, small spoons. Try plastics for early smallmouth at Lambton Woods.
- **Small-stream tactics (Mad / Boyne / Sheldon / Innisfil / Pine / Sydenham):** 6'6"–7'6" UL/L spinning, 4lb mono. Mepps #0/1, Panther Martins, worm under split shot. Light tackle; tight casting.
- **Gear (steelhead rivers):** 8'6"–10'6" ML steelhead rod, 6lb mainline, 6lb fluoro leader.
- **Cautions:**
  - **Light drizzle both days (0.6mm)** — drizzle, not blow-out. Slight water stain may actually improve the bite.
  - **Sun dawn 5.7°C** — cooler than Sat (8.1°C); thermos and de-icer worth packing if you go Sun.
  - **Last Quarter moon** = balanced activity; standard dawn-patrol applies on both days.
  - **Beaver is popular** — arrive at first light or work mouth/Heathcote if shoulder-to-shoulder.
  - **Tue May 5 rain** is moderate-to-heavy (7.9mm) — won't blow rivers out by Sat (4 days of recovery), but expect levels slightly above today's snapshot.
- **Regulations:** Always verify on [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary). Lower year-round + FMZ 16/17 inland all open. **Notty between Boyne and Pine confluences (incl. Essa Centennial Park) = C&R rainbow only.**

## Notes

- **FMZ 16 inland trout regs:** open since Apr 25; standard creel limits. MNR brook-trout headwaters are low-density populations — **consider barbless C&R**.

- **Tackle swap:** small-stream brook trout = 6'6"–7'6" UL/L spinning, 4lb mono, small spinners (Mepps #0/1, Panther Martin) or worm under split shot. Steelhead rod is overkill upstream.
- **Sequencing for Sat May 9:**
