### What to Expect

- **Species:** Drop-back steelhead at Twenty Mile mouth and Lynn mouth; resident rainbow/brown trout in upper Twenty Mile (above QEW); lake-staging fish at Big Creek NWA.
- **Twenty Mile tactics:** Float roe (chartreuse, pink, white) at Jordan Harbour mouth at first light. Move to QEW bridge if mouth gets crowded. Inline spinners (Mepps #2) and small spoons (Cleos #2) work in deeper holes.
- **Lynn tactics:** Cast spoons across Port Dover harbour for staging fish; float roe at the mouth.
- **Gear:** 8'6"–10'6" ML steelhead rod, 6lb mainline, 6lb fluoro leader. Waders helpful for cross-stream casting on Twenty Mile lower.
- **Cautions:**
  - **Cold dawn (1.5°C Sun)** — ice on guides; thermos.
  - **Full moon** — softer dawn bite likely; mid-morning push (8–10am).
  - **Twenty Mile harbour mouth is exposed** — check wind forecast Sat night.
- **Regulations:** Always verify on [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary). Twenty Mile lower (QEW → Lake Ontario): FMZ 16, no close season, S-5 / C-2 rainbow/brown.
