# Go Fishing Here — Overall

## 2026-04-29 — Multi-zone summary (Fri May 1, Sat May 2, Sun May 3, 2026)

### Best zone for the window: **West**

West gauges are the most consistently dropping — Credit -2.01m, Bronte -0.70m, 16 Mile -0.12m, Etobicoke -0.53m, Grand -23.79m. Closest big-water access (Credit at Streetsville/Erindale, 30–40 min drive). Drop-back steelhead at Port Credit mouth on the full-moon trailing morning (Sun May 3). North runs a close second — Humber is 10–20 min and dropping fast.

### Run status across zones

Drop-back phase is broadly winding down. Confirmed counter data shows zero net passage everywhere with active counters: Ganaraska Riverwatcher 0 (Apr 1–29), Bowmanville BRAVO Node 22 STANDBY 0, Beaver/Thornbury BRAVO Node 10 0 since Mar 25 run start (water 13.0°C). The Credit CRAA camera is online without counts. Anecdotal and historical data still suggest active drop-back fish at lake mouths and below dam structures (Goodyear, Greenwood, Corbett's, Thornbury, Denny's). Resident rainbow/brown trout active across all FMZ 17 inland waters now that the season is open.

### Pick-the-zone logic

- **Want shortest drive + reliable big water:** North → Humber (10–20 min)
- **Want best gauge data + walkable runs:** West → Credit (30–40 min)
- **Want unique water + permissive regs:** Southwest → Twenty Mile (55–65 min, no close season)
- **Want your usual producer Bowmanville:** East — but check 02HD006 gauge Sat night before driving. If still rising, switch zones.
