## New Spots — Southwest (cluster: Aylmer, ~30 min radius)

Cluster centered on Aylmer yields three under-fished Southwest candidates plus a fourth mouth-access option, all off the year-round exclusion roster and open May 1–3. Spots leverage road-allowance bridges, gauge-supported flow checks, and lower-pressure dynamics compared to Big Creek or Lynn; drives from Toronto range 1h 35m–2h 15m, requiring early departure or overnight planning.

## Notes

- **FMZ 17 inland trout regs:** open since 4th Sat April; standard creel limits. Verify FMZ 16 boundary if fishing Niagara-region creeks.
- **Property lines:** road-allowance only at bridges. Don't cross fences.
- **Tackle swap:** small Lake Erie tribs = 8'6"–9'6" ML for steelhead at mouths, 7' UL/L for resident browns upstream.
- **Sequencing for Sun May 3:** Hit Twenty Mile (Jordan Harbour) at first light → Nanticoke Creek mouth mid-morning if energy and time allow (1h 35m drive south).
