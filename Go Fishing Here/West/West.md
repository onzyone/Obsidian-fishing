### What to Expect

West rebounded hard. Drop-back steelhead window is closing — expect resident browns + a tail of post-spawn hens at the small-creek mouths. Tactics: small spinners (Mepps #1–2), drift-rigged roe bags or single eggs, tan/olive woolly buggers on a 4–6 wt fly rod. Lake-mouth fish on clear water are spooky — go finer leader (6–8lb fluoro) and longer drifts. Bump tippet to 8–10lb if water gets coloured post-rain. With the 🌗 Last Quarter (49%) the bite curve is standard — hit dawn patrol hard. Saturday rain hits Toronto at midday so fish dawn and bail by 10am.
