### New Spots Explore

#### New Spots — East (cluster: Pickering / West Duffins, ~30 min radius)

Cluster anchored on West Duffins / Pickering — three under-fished spots inside ~45–60 min of 427 & Finch. Whitevale (West Duffins) holds resident brookies above the dam barrier; Cobourg upper requires longer drive but accesses cold-water steelhead refuges; Soper Creek Trail mid-section pairs naturally with a Bowmanville dawn run.

## Notes

- **FMZ 17 inland trout regs:** open since Apr 25; standard creel limits. MNR brook-trout headwaters are low-density populations — **consider barbless C&R**.
- **Property lines:** road-allowance access only on most upper pins. Stay in the road allowance, don't cross fences. Many upper sections are private.
- **Tackle swap:** small-stream brook trout = 6'6"–7'6" UL/L spinning, 4lb mono, small spinners (Mepps #0/1, Panther Martin) or worm under split shot. Steelhead rod is overkill upstream.
- **Pre-trip check:** Tue/Wed rain event may bump Whitevale and Soper temporarily — verify Bowmanville (02HD006) and Duffins (02HC049) gauges Friday before driving.
