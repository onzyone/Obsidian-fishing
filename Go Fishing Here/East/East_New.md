### New Spots Explore

#### New Spots — East (cluster: Newcastle / Clarington, ~30 min radius, 55–65 min drive)

Brook-trout-heavy small-stream cluster on the Newcastle / Clarington side — clears 1–2 days fast, FMZ 17 inland open since Apr 25, road-allowance access only on most of these (private property dominates). Pair them with a morning hit on Wilmot mouth or Bowmanville Goodyear Dam pool, then push upstream/sideways to these small headwaters mid-day.

**Notes:**
- **FMZ 17 inland trout regs:** open since 4th Sat April; standard creel limits. MNR brook-trout headwaters are low-density populations — consider barbless C&R.
- **Property lines:** road-allowance access only on these pins. Stay in the road allowance, don't cross fences. Many upper sections are private.
- **Tackle swap:** small-stream brook trout = 6'6"–7'6" UL/L spinning, 4lb mono, small spinners (Mepps #0/1, Panther Martin) or worm under split shot. Steelhead rod is overkill upstream.
