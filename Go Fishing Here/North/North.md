### What to Expect

- **Species:** Drop-back rainbow/brown trout (Beaver, Saugeen, Notty mouth); resident species (Mad, upper sections); some chinook holdovers (Beaver historic, Saugeen archive).
- **Humber tactics:** Float fishing through Old Mill / Etienne Brulé pools — single egg bags (chartreuse/pink), roe sacs, small spoons (Cleos #2). Lake-mouth at Humber Bay for staging fish.
- **Beaver tactics:** Float roe through Thornbury runs below the ladder; drop-backs hold here. Be ready to share — this is a known spot.
- **Nottawasaga tactics:** Float roe at Wasaga mouth; cast spoons across the harbour for staging fish.
- **Gear:** 8'6"–10'6" ML steelhead rod, 6lb mainline, 6lb fluoro leader. Bring de-icer.
- **Cautions:**
  - **Cold dawn (1.5°C Sun)** — ice on guides; thermos.
  - **Full moon overnight Sat** — softer dawn bite likely; mid-morning push (8–10am).
  - **Beaver is popular** — arrive at first light or pick a less-known spot.
- **Regulations:** Always verify on [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary). FMZ 16/17 lower sections all open since Apr 25.
