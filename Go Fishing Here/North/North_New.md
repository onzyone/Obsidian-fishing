### New Spots Explore

#### New Spots — North (cluster: Owen Sound / Sydenham River, ~30 min radius)

Owen Sound cluster on the **Sydenham River** — long drive (~2h 20–45 min from 427 & Finch) but four legitimate spots inside a ~15 min radius means you can rotate quickly if the first one is dead. Mill Dam holds migratory drop-backs below the falls; Inglis Falls is above all migratory barriers (resident brookies/browns only). FMZ 16 inland open since Apr 25.

## Notes

- **FMZ 16 inland trout regs:** open since Apr 25; standard creel limits. MNR brook-trout headwaters are low-density populations — **consider barbless C&R**.
- **Property lines:** road-allowance access only on most upper Sydenham pins. Stay in the road allowance, don't cross fences. Many upper sections are private.
- **Tackle swap:** small-stream brook trout = 6'6"–7'6" UL/L spinning, 4lb mono, small spinners (Mepps #0/1, Panther Martin) or worm under split shot. Steelhead rod is overkill upstream.
- **Owen Sound day** is its own commitment — full Sydenham loop (Harbour Marina → Mill Dam → Harrison Park → Inglis Falls) is a 4-stop sweep but burns 5h+ in driving total. Save for a focused day, not a Beaver/Humber backup.
