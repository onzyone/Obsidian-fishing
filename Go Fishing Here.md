### What to Expect

- **Species:** Drop-back steelhead (lake-bound, aggressive at mouths and below dams/ladders), resident rainbow/brown trout, suckers stacking. Bowmanville also gets some chinook holdover and occasional Atlantic salmon.
- **Bowmanville tactics:** Float fishing with single-egg bags (chartreuse, pink, white) or roe sacs through the runs/pools between Spry Ave and the dam. Small spoons (Little Cleos #2, Krocodiles in silver/blue or fire tiger) and inline spinners (Mepps #2, Blue Fox) work in deeper holes. Hit prime zone at first light, expand to King St / Roenigk after 8am.
- **Ganaraska tactics:** Same float setup; spring drop-backs hammer roe at Sylvan Glen pools and below Corbett's Dam. Be ready to walk — the Ganny is bigger water and crowds concentrate at obvious access points.
- **Duffins tactics:** Below Greenwood dam = float roe through the deep run; drop-backs stack here under full moon. Bayly St mouth area for lake-staging fish.
- **Gear:** 8'6"–10'6" ML steelhead rod, centerpin or spinning reel, 6lb mono mainline (or 8lb braid), 6lb fluoro leader. Waders helpful for cross-stream coverage but not strictly needed for the prime Bowmanville zone (gravel trail right along creek).
- **Cautions:**
  - **Cold mornings (1–2°C Sat, 1.7°C Sun)** — ice on guides at first light; bring de-icer or thermos with hot water; fingerless wool gloves help.
  - **Full moon overnight feed** — softer dawn bite likely; have a mid-morning push plan (8–10am second wave).
  - **Friday drizzle** — fishable but bring rain shell; light stain often helps bite, but if drizzle thickens to steady rain check evening window instead.
  - **Sanctuaries** — Bowmanville fish ladder (Goodyear Dam) is sanctuary; Ganaraska Hwy 401 → Jocelyn St Bridge is sanctuary year-round. Verify signage onsite.
- **Regulations:** Always verify on [ontario.ca](https://www.ontario.ca/document/ontario-fishing-regulations-summary). FMZ 17 lower sections all open since Apr 25. Standard limits — no C&R-only zones on Bowmanville, Wilmot, Oshawa, or Ganaraska open sections.
